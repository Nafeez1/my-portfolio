HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PICTURE
================================

1. Save your profile picture as "profile.jpg" in the public folder
   - The image you shared should be saved as: public/profile.jpg

2. Alternatively, you can use these formats:
   - profile.jpg
   - profile.png
   - profile.jpeg

3. The image will automatically appear in the Hero section of your portfolio

4. Recommended image specifications:
   - Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait)
   - Minimum size: 600x800 pixels
   - Maximum size: 1200x1600 pixels
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - File size: Under 500KB for faster loading

5. After adding the image, refresh your browser to see it

CURRENT STATUS:
- Hero section is configured to use /profile.jpg
- Fallback placeholder will show if image is not found
- Image has hover effects and 3D tilt interaction
